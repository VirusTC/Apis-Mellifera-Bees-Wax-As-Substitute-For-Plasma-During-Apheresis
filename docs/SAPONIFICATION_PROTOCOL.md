# Organic Saponification Protocol: Converting Wax Matrix to Aqueous Plasma

Raw *Apis mellifera* beeswax is inherently hydrophobic and cannot be introduced directly into the vascular system. This clinical protocol outlines the saponification process required to hydrolyze organic beeswax into water-soluble fatty acid salts capable of functioning as a plasma-volume expander scaffold.

## 1. Organic Input Specifications
To maintain an entire organic supply chain without synthetic chemical residues, all inputs must meet the following parameters:
*   **Wax Base:** Certified organic, unbleached, triple-filtered *Apis mellifera* cappings wax (free of synthetic pesticides or acaricides).
*   **Alkaline Agent:** Organic-certified potassium hydroxide (KOH) derived from the leaching of organic hardwood ash, or food-grade organic-certified lye.
*   **Buffering Medium:** Organic food-grade citric acid (derived from citrus fermentation) or organic cider vinegar for pH titration.

## 2. Chemical Hydrolysis Parameters
The reaction breaks down the wax esters into long-chain fatty acid potassium salts and free alcohols:

$$\text{C}_{15}\text{H}_{31}\text{COOC}_{30}\text{H}_{61} (\text{Myricil Palmitate}) + \text{KOH} \rightarrow \text{C}_{15}\text{H}_{31}\text{COOK} (\text{Potassium Palmitate}) + \text{C}_{30}\text{H}_{61}\text{OH} (\text{Myricil Alcohol})$$

### Operational Parameters:
*   **Reaction Temperature:** Maintain strictly between **82°C – 85°C** (above the melting point of beeswax but below the boiling point of the water carrier).
*   **Saponification Value:** Target **89–103 mg KOH / g wax** to ensure complete ester conversion.
*   **Agitation Rate:** Continuous high-shear mixing at **1,200 RPM** for a duration of 180 minutes.

## 3. Post-Reaction Titration and Purification
The crude saponified mixture is highly alkaline (pH >11.0) and highly cytotoxic. It must be brought to physiological norms:
1.  **Neutralization:** Gradually titrate the mixture with an organic citric acid buffer solution until the pH stabilizes exactly between **7.35 and 7.45**.
2.  **Methanol/Ethanol-Free Precipitation:** Centrifuge the solution at **4,000 × g** for 45 minutes to precipitate and remove any unreacted, insoluble myricil alcohols.
3.  **Sterile Filtration:** Pass the neutralized aqueous phase through a sterile **0.22-micron polyethersulfone (PES) filter** under a laminar flow hood to yield a sterile, water-soluble plasma scaffold.
